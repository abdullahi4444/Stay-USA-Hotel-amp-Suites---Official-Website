All Guests Enjoy


Providing Guests with Convenient On-Site Perks


We like to welcome each and every guest to the USA Stay Hotel & Suites in our trademark warm style. From the moment you
enter our doors you will feel our commitment to service and our desire to make your stay as comfortable as possible. With a
fitness room we’re one of the most convenient and affordable hotels in Hot Springs.

Our front desk staff is also here for you at any time to provide recommendations for things to do in the area and Hot Springs
attractions, offer suggestions on where to dine and restaurants, and fulfill most any request you have.
Additionally, Woolly’s Western Grill is located across the highway from us for convenience and good food, featuring Mexican
and American food. Home of the famous “Baby Woolly”.

The USA Stay Hotel & Suites here in Hot Springs, South Dakota truly provides one of the best values in the Black Hills region
and is committed to making your stay a warm and welcoming one.
