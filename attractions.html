SOUTH DAKOTA


Attractions


Located in Hot Springs, South Dakota, make Stay USA Hotel & Suites your base while visiting the spectacular Black Hills National Forest
and Custer State Park where buffalo still roam. Visit the Prehistoric Mammoth Site located near our hotel and Wind Cave National Park, a
complex maze of caves located below the prairie. Other area attractions include Angostura Recreation Area and Windcross Conservancy.

Mount Rushmore
National Monument


Mount Rushmore National Memorial is a sculpture carved into the
granite face of Mount Rushmore near Keystone, South Dakota, in the
United States.
Mount Rushmore features 60-foot sculptures of the heads of former
United States presidents (in order from left to right) George Washington,
Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Roosevelt and Abraham Lincoln. The entire
memorial covers 1,278.45 acres and is 5,725 feet above sea level.
(56 miles)
Website




Custer State Park


Custer State Park is a South Dakota State Park and wildlife reserve in the
Black Hills, United States. The park is South Dakota’s largest and first
state park, named after Lt. Colonel George Armstrong Custer.
(21 miles)
Website




Prehistoric
Mammoth Site


The Mammoth Site in Hot Springs, South Dakota, is the world’s largest
mammoth research facility. You can tour an active paleontological dig
site and view Ice Age fossils exhibited as they are found. Open year
round, the Mammoth Site offers the museum visitor a 30-minute guided
tour plus a 10-minute video. Tour information features the Mammoth
Site and Ice Age geology, paleontology, and paleoecology. Today, visitors
to the museum observe first-hand a scientific excavation.
(.3 miles)
Website

Angostura Recreation
Area


Angostura Reservoir is a water-lover’s haven with breathtaking, scenic
views. Offering crystal clear waters, 36 miles of shoreline, and some of
the finest sandy beaches in the state, the area boasts many water sports
and summer fun activities – camping, boating, fishing, and swimming.
(9.8 miles)
Website



Wildcross
Conservatory


Located near Hot Springs, the Windcross Spanish Mustang Preserve is one of the only surviving foundation Spanish Mustang herds in America. The Wild Horse Sanctuary is where visitors can see hundreds of wild mustangs and their foals roaming free across wind swept prairies. Travel back in time and view the rugged natural beauty of America as it was 100 years ago. Explore wild horse history and learn how important horses were to early man. (13.3 miles)
