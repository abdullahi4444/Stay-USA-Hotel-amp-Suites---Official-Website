Accessibility Statement



Our goal is to provide outstanding hospitality to all travelers and their endeavors, this includes everyone. Our mission is to provide opportunities to all, including those with hearing, vision, or any disability to best understand our products and services before making a booking decision. Any questions or concerns can be taken up with our friendly staff via telephone or email.

We strive to provide a user-friendly online experience to all guests, regardless of their device type. Guests who require assistive technology such as a screen reader, font magnification, translation or voice recognition software can find comfort in knowing their needs will be fulfilled. We are committed to compliance with Level AA success criteria of the Website Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0 (WCAG 2.0), an international standard measure of accessibility success. These particular guidelines are carefully monitored to continue our pursuit of a user-friendly online experience. Our website has been tested to ensure we meet or exceed Level AA checkpoints of the WCAG 2.0 Standard.

Our website allows travelers to search for accessibility featured rooms and provides details about our property’s accessibility features. The appropriate policies, best practices, and procedures are in place to ensure that individuals with disabilities can locate and make reservations for accessible guest rooms with ease.

As part of our reservation processes, we:

Ensure that accessible guest rooms are held for use by individuals with disabilities until all other guest rooms of that type have been rented and the accessible room requested is the only remaining room of that type;

Reserve, upon request, accessible guest rooms or specific types of guest rooms and ensure that the guest rooms requested are blocked and removed from all reservations systems; and

Guarantee that accessible guest rooms reserved through our system are held for the guest, regardless of other inquiries. If you require any assistance with booking your reservation, please contact us directly via email, phone, or social media, which you will find on our contact page. If you have any questions or would like to report any issues related to the accessibility features of our hotel’s website, please contact us at (619) 435-4131 or frontdesk@elcordovahotel.com.

Learn more about W3C’s Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI) https://www.w3.org/WAI/

WEBSITE ACCESSIBILITY

This website is designed to be fully accessible to all and according to W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 conforms to most priority checkpoints which entail some of the following elements:

NAVIGATION SHORTCUTS

Our hotel’s website can be navigated by using the tab key to traverse the elements of each page and by using the enter key to activate a highlighted link.

STRUCTURED, SEMANTIC MARKUP

HTML heading tags are used to convey the document structure. Navigation menus are tagged as HTML maps so that the menu title can be read out, and the menu items are presented as a group.

IMAGES AVAILABILITY

All content images include descriptive ALT attributes, and all purely decorative graphics include null ALT attributes.

STANDARDS COMPLIANCE

The site is designed to meet expectations of Level AA compliance as specified by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines. All pages validate as XHTML 1.0 and use structured semantic markup.
